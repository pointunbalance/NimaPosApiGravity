I have located and fixed the issue where selecting specific menus/tabs (such as target button 11 "السلوك والتربية" (Behavioral Tracking) or button 12 "المهام الإدارية" (Administrative Checklist)) did not show their respective data lists but instead showed a generic placeholder interface.

### Root Cause
In `/components/school/students/SchoolStudentModal.tsx`, there is a conditional display of the "هذا القسم قيد التطوير..." (This section is under development) placeholder banner for tabs that are not listed in the exclusion array. The checklist (`checklist`) and behavioral (`behavioral`) tabs were fully coded and functional, but they were missing from the tab exclusion list. Clicking them would overlay the "Under Development" placeholder on top of their actual forms.

### Changes Made
1. **Updated Tab Active Exclusions:**
   - Modified `/components/school/students/SchoolStudentModal.tsx` to include `"behavioral"` and `"checklist"` inside the activeTab exclusion list.
2. **Verified Build:**
   - Compiled the application using the build command to ensure 100% success and no compilation errors.
