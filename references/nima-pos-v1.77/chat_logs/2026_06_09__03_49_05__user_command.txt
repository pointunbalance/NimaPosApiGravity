📝 البرومبت المقترح (Prompt for UI/UX Enhancement)
Role: Senior UI/UX Specialist & Frontend Architect.
Task: Provide architectural layout adjustments and visual styling enhancements for an enterprise-grade Restaurant/Retail POS web application based on a modern light theme aesthetic.

Design Objectives & Enhancements:

Visual Hierarchy & Grid Optimization:

Card Layout: Refine the product grid cards. Introduce subtle elevation drops (soft box-shadows) instead of flat borders to create depth. Ensure a clearer separation between the product details (Name, Category, Stock level) and the price tag.

Typography Tuning: Enhance font-weight contrast. Make product titles slightly bolder, and give the stock indicators (متوفر: X) a pill-shaped, soft-colored background (e.g., light green pastel for available, soft gray for out-of-stock) to improve rapid scanning.

Duplicate Elements: Clean up or optimize any accidental grid repetitions to show a broader variety of product states (e.g., active, selected, hovered, disabled/out of stock).

Sidebar & Cart Experience (Right-to-Left / RTL):

Total Section: Make the final total price (الإجمالي) stand out more aggressively, as it is the most crucial data point during checkout. Consider giving it a distinct background container or a slightly larger, high-contrast typography.

Action Buttons: The checkout button (دفع) should feel heavy and tactile. Add a slight hover state transition concept. For secondary action buttons (hold, print, notes), use precise, micro-interactions or modern minimal outlines to avoid visual clutter.

Customer Selection: Refine the customer profile dropdown/input area to make it look integrated, utilizing clean input fields with floating labels or distinct icons.

Top Utility Bar & Categorization:

Category Pills/Tabs: The horizontal category filter tabs (الكل, الوجبات, etc.) need clear active vs. inactive states. Suggest a sleek design where the active tab uses a bold brand accent color, while inactive tabs use a neutral, soft background.

Action Bar Icons: Clean up the top row of utility buttons. Ensure consistent padding, sizing, and icon weights across all quick-action utilities (barcode, scale, history, settings) to establish an enterprise-grade, polished feel.

Color Palette & Contrast:

Maintain the crisp "Modern Light Theme" baseline (clean white backgrounds #FFFFFF paired with soft grays #F8F9FA or #F4F5F7 for structural sections).

Introduce a highly professional secondary accent color for critical indicators (e.g., alert states, special discounts, or specific toggles) to break visual monotony safely.

Output Requirement: Provide structured textual recommendations, UI/UX logic, and precise design prompts to achieve these enhancements. Do not output raw code.
