I have successfully designed and built the Device Location & Shelf Tracking sub-system ("لوجستيات حركة الجهاز والرفوف") within the computer and mobile maintenance module:

1. **Shelf/Bin Mapping ("نظام تكويد الرفوف")**:
   - Expanded `MaintenanceOrder` in `types.ts` with `shelfCode?: string` to link each maintenance ticket securely with its physical shelf location.
   - Built an interactive, dynamic workshop shelf map visualizer that groups shelves into logical workflow zones (Received, Testing, Under Repair, Ready for Delivery, Delivered/Archived).
   - Shows current capacity configurations, actual counts, and real-time occupancy percentages.

2. **Quick Location Scanner & Updater ("تحديث الموقع السريع")**:
   - Implemented a simulated laser barcode scanner tool where employees scan the device barcode (e.g. `DEV-1`) and then scan the shelf/bin barcode (e.g. `SHELF-C2`) to relocate devices instantly with simulated success/failure buzzer beeps.
   - Provided a manual assignment dropdown fallback for desktop users to match devices to shelves directly.
   - Logs live scanner streams inside a retro, active terminal log console showing success/err feedback streams.

3. **Sticker and Label Generator ("طباعة ملصق رف")**:
   - Built a dynamic print page generation routine to print QR/barcoded shelf stickers and custom device maintenance ticket tags on thermal POS label printers.

4. **Sidebar Navigation Integration**:
   - Added `computer-mobile-maintenance/shelves` as a designated route inside `App.tsx` and configured it neatly in `/components/layout/navigationConfig.ts` with the `MapPin` icon, fully responsive and styled on a clean, day-theme visual aesthetic.
