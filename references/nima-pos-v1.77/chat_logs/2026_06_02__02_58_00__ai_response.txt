   - **Smart Moving Average Cost Calculations:** Formulated mathematical recalculations of Moving Average Cost on items when saving incoming supplier invoices:
     `Moving Average Cost = ((Current Stock * Previous Average Cost) + (Purchased Qty * Purchased Cost)) / (Current Stock + Purchased Qty)`
     Ensures smart, real-time inventory ledger valuations.
